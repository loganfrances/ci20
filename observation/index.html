numark mixtrack pro III

Overview of controller.

Fader Control: Control which channel is heard on a sliding scale. Does not affect the volume level of each channel, instead controls the amount of audio being output to sound system by each channel.

Filter Knob + Level Controls: Rotating knobs to control filtering of each channel, as well as the treble, midtones, and bass.

Channel Volume Control: Controlling the individual volume level of each channel. Does no affect volume of output to headphones -- only output to sound system.

Wheel: Rotating disc used for scratching, beat matching, and mixing.

Feedback: Plug-in feedback.

Loaded Track Feedback: Play/Pause button lights up green when a track is loaded into a channel.

Headphone Toggle: Button for sending audio to the headphones for pre-cueing. Headphone audio is controlled by the CUE MIX + CUE GAIN knobs in the center of the controller.

Pad Menu Toggle: Pad menu is toggled with the PAD MODE button. Pad modes include: Manual Looping, Auto Looping, and Samples. There is a fourth button and I'm really not sure why it's there to be honest.

Scratch Toggling: Toggle for scratch effects on the wheel is togglled by the WHEEL button.

Headphone Input Port: Headpone input made for male audio connections.

Audio Output Jack + Power USB Port: RCA Audio outpot port, and power connection for the board.
