step three

now match the song's BPM on the deck that is not playing to the BPM of the song that is playing. this can be done with the slider or the sync button. we'll call this track the transition track.

step four

slide the channel fader on the transition deck's side all the way to zero. this will prevent the channel from outputing to the speakers, but will allow you to start the track when you need.

step five

wait until the active track gets to the place you want to transition. when that happens, you can either loop the track to give you more time to prepare, or immediately start adding the transition track.begin playing the transition track on the opposite deck, and start slowly turning up the channel fader.

step six

now that you've begun to introduce the transition track, begin simultaneously exiting the active track by either turning hown the channel fader, or filtering out the song using the filter knob. after either of those options are maxed out, pause the song, and finish turning up the transition track.
